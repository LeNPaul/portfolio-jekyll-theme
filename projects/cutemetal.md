Now, before you start jumping up and down, let me say this upfront: Cute Metal is not a thing as such, not an actual genre, no matter how much <a href="https://www.dictionary.com/e/slang/weeb/" target="_blank" rel="noopener"><span>weebs</span></a> try to convince you it is, but more of a description of the type of music and stage shows of cutesy bands from all over the world. I'm not only thinking here of **Babymetal**, who pioneered Kawaii Metal in Japan in the early 2010s, a genre that combines the noisy barrage of Heavy Metal instrumentation with J-pop melodies and idol aesthetics and vocals -- I'm also thinking here of **Charamel**, a Visual Kei band comprised of only *yuru-chara* (i.e. mascot characters), among them internet celebrity **Nyango Starr**, an apple-themed mascot who is one sick drummer. And looking beyond Japan, and if we stretch the meaning of "Heavy Metal" sufficiently, the alternative emos of the Slovakian band **Pentagramƒçek** deserve a mention too. They play Satanic versions of children's songs.
